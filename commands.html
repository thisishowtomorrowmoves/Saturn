Commands

.shelp

Displays all commands and usage info.

.ban [@user] [reason]

Bans a user from the server.

.unban [userID]

Checks Saturn's response time.

.kick [@user] [reason]

Kicks a member from the server.

.mute [@user] [reason]

Mutes a member for 10hr-1wk.

.unmute [@user]

Unmutes a member.

.roleadd [@user] [@role]

Grants a user a role.

.roleremove [@user] [@role]

Removes a users role.

.purge [ammount]

Mass deletes messages, e.g .purge 100.

.login [LastFMuser]

Logs the bot into your fm account.

.fm

Shows your last played song.

.ping

Checks Saturn's response time.

.botinfo

Gives info on the bot.
